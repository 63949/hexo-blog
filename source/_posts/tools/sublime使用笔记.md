## 默认使用4个空格代替tab
打开`Preferences -> Settings-User`，添加：

```
"tab_size": 4,
"translate_tabs_to_spaces": true,
```

[sublime text3设定TAB键为4个空格 | 千思网](http://www.qiansw.com/sublime-text3%E8%AE%BE%E5%AE%9Atab%E9%94%AE%E4%B8%BA4%E4%B8%AA%E7%A9%BA%E6%A0%BC.html)
